First self-financed Engineering College in composite state of Andhra Pradesh established in the year 1977 and first private college to offer PG Programmes in engineering in Andhra Pradesh in the year 1998. Offering 8 UG (B.Tech) Courses with intake of 1140, 9 M.Tech (Specialisations) -108, MBA-60, MCA-60. Total Sudent Strength in the campus- 5421. Autonomous College by UGC since 2006 & extended for 10 years, upto 2027-28 without visit to the college, first in A.P. Funded with Rs. 6 Crores by World Bank and MHRD under TEQIP-II S.C.1.2 (Scaling up of PG Education and Demand Driven R,D & I)NIRF Ranking- The college is ranked among the top 200 engineering institutions in the country for the lass three consecutive years and got a rank of 156 in 2020. The College obtained ISO 9001:2000 certification initially in the year 2004 and has been renewing it. Subsequently, the college received ISO 9001:2015 certification after achieving autonomous status. Recognized as ‘Scientific & Industrial Research Organization (SIRO)’ by DSIR, MST Government of India since August, 2017 AICTE-CII Survey- Received Platinum Award in years 2017, 2018 , 2019 & 2020 as a Best Industry Linked Technical Institute. NPTEL/SWAYM- The institute secured AAA ranking and all India 7th position for the participation by students and faculty. Only private college select for MARGADARSHAN scheme by AICTE in A.P. and Telangana. 319 Total Faculty with 134 Ph.D.s, 137 pursuing Ph.D.s Strong Alumni Base- support in the form of Scholarships for academically bright and needy students, Student Research & Innovation. Majority of Senior Faculty members have visited foreign universities in various countries for presenting research papers in Canada, Australia, Germany, Malaysia, USA, Japan, China, Austria, Switzerland, Russia and Singapore. Collaboration with 7 foreign universities in USA and Australia. 60 MoUs with Industry Partners and Internship in 130 Companies Adjudged as one of the Best Engineering Colleges in the Country by National Survey agencies such as Out Look, Data Quest, Competitive Success Review, Careers 360 and Higher Education Review etc. In house hostel accommodation to 1070 students. NCC- The college houses 1 (A) EME COY NCC. The cadets of the college unit have been securing many medals and prize at various training campus. The cadets have been participating in activities like Trekking, Mountaineering. Apart from regular NCC activities, the cadets participate in various service oriented activities. NSS- The College was selected for the prestigious “Unnata Bhara Abhyan” Scheme under the aegis of MHRD & AICTE to serve 5 villages i.e. China Ogirala, Kasaranenivaripalem, Chodavaram, Kanuru and Ammenapuram. Received appreciating award for blood donation from Hon’ble Governor, Sri E S L Narasimhan, ‘Savior of Life’ award from Red Cross Society, ‘Chatrapati Award’ from Shivaji Foundation and prizes for ‘Digital India’ and in NSS youth festival. 42% of power required for the college needs is generated using 400 kWp roof top solar plant installed with an investment of Rs. 2 Crores.
